Fire Facts


  • A home fire occurs every 88 seconds in the United States

    Home fires are responsible for between 10-11,000 injuries every year with the leading injury being smoke inhalation.
  • Over age 65 you are two times more likely to die in fire than general population.

    Smoke deaths outnumber fire deaths 8:1

  • There is still a 20% mortality rate with a fully functional sprinkler system
  • Egress ladders are bulky and difficult to deploy by children, elderly and people with disabilities

Features

The Smoke Shield System provides breathable air in the event of a fire for up to 1 hour

Carbon Capture Filter

Our filter uses high grade activated charcoal pellets in a customized mesh to filter out toxins in smoke.

Automated Activation

A built in sensor detects smoke in the air during a fire, and automatically starts the Smoke Shield System.

Mounting System

Our units are specially designed so that anyone is able to install them. A total installation takes less than 30 minutes.
