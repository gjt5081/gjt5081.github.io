Breathe easier. Your family has time.


Our product is an affordable, automated, and easy to install system that provides peace of mind knowing there is an additional layer of fire protection for your loved ones.


The Smoke Shield System provides up to one hour of breathable air in a fire if escape becomes impossible. Two specialized filter compartments are built into the system to remove toxic gases in smoke. It is battery operated, installable in any standard closet, and automatically starts in the presence of smoke.


Where can the system be installed?

Any space where escape from a fire may be difficult.

Who does this benefit?

Anyone in need of escape from a fire can benefit from the Smoke Shield System.

Why choose the Smoke Shield System?

  • The Smoke Shield System provides up to 1 hour of breathable air in a fire if escape is not possible

  • An LED strobe light sits outside of the closet to alert others of system activation

  • High speed fans on each side of the system enable air flow and filtration of smoke toxins

  • Designed for most standard closets



    • Automated Activation

    Carbon Capture Filter

    Our speciality filter uses a blend of catalyst and activated charcoal pellets in a customized mesh to filter out toxins in smoke.
